Our Story

FoodIncs opened its first store in 2021 in Polokwane. At the time it was known as FoodIncs Seshego Surbabs. This store was one of the first outlets in South Africa to offer Mexican Dishes. The FoodIncs Chicken Villages concept is based on “Mexican Flavoured Flame Grilled Chicken” combined with a specialised Mexican Foods menu. The motivating factor for this choice was the demand for consumers for “exotic” and healthy dining. Store designs are funky and modern and create the perfect ambiance for fun and comfortable dining. .
